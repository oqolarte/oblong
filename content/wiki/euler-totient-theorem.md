If *n* and *a* are coprime positive integers,
and \( φ(n) \) is [Euler's totient function](/euler-totient-function),
then *a* raised to the power of \( φ(n) \) is congruent to 1 modulo *n*;
that is:

\[
a^φ(n) ≡ 1 (mod n)
\]
