## Generous trees

All trees are generous. We are blessed to have a few trees in our lot.

### Avocado

One of them is an avocado tree. And like most avocado trees these days,
they are blooming and bearing. The fruits, though, get "aborted" before
they grow to the size we see in markets. That's fine; when placed beside
a bunch of bananas, they ripe quickly anyway.

### Duhat

Another fruit-bearing tree is duhat. So generous that we are showered
(love-bombed?) with its fruits. Unlike the avocado, though, no one pays
attention to its fruits. Previous logs about this specific duhat
tree, in which I was not exactly being grateful by its generosity:
