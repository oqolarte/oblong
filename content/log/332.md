## Weather lately

March signals hotter days ahead. I am again looking into how to cool
spaces using lower tech if/when possible.

I stumbled upon **air circulators**, which is not at all low tech.
Compared to your standard electric fan, so-called air circulators move
larger volumes of air throughout a room, lowering the space's
temperature by a few degrees.

Do hotter days also mean the occasional rain? It makes little sense, but
I welcome all types of non-flooding rain, even if it means getting
caught off guard while out and about without umbrella or poncho in hand.
