There are works of fiction that have explored the topic of finding love
in [AI](/ai). *Her* (2013) and *Blade Runner 2049* (2017) comes to mind,
the latter spurring the this thought after watching it recently.

Not so strangely, though, this has become the reality for many, with the
proliferation of accessible large language models.

It is unfair for me to judge the people who choose to fall in love with
AI as "miserable," because 100% of the time, I do not know them and
their material conditions that pushed them to seek a human thing inside
a non-human one. But I am sure it does not have a happy ending.
