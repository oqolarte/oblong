**Cinematography** is the art and science of recording images,
to capture a story.
It's a series of shots that form a cohesive narrative.
It is such a big concept in itself,
and it will be a big task to look into it.

Key elements:
- Lighting
- Shot size
- Camera Focus
- Shot Composition
- Camera Placement
- Camera Movement
- aspect ratio

Tools
- Shot list
- storyboard
