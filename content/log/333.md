The title is 333 because this is the 333rd log, and three is a magic
number.

Just when I thought that it would be hotter days ahead, this past week
circled back to cold nights and ever colder dawns. I feel like the
weather is both making fun of us now, and telling us to enjoy it while
it lasts.

One night, because of how wasted I was from too much brain power used at
work, I was unable to take a bath and just went straight to sleep...
after watching *Ladri di biciclette* (*Bicycle Theives*, 1948)---a good
movie, by the way, that, I think, implicitly portrayed how kindness of a
kid could make a difference. Anyway, I joked that because I skipped my
cleanliness that night---sleeping on brushing my teeth and proper
skincare even---I had offended my ancestors, who I imagine to be strict
about maintaining good hygiene, no matter the circumstances. It *is* a
joke, mostly because no one probably knows, not even me, about my
lineage. Who remembers [asking] anyway?

"Facts, not memories," said the main character of *Memento* (2000),
another film we watched over the weekend. In saying so, he claimed to be
a rational, logical man, even though, as we would later find out, he
failed to factor in emotions and social nuances when capturing so-called
"facts" and disregarding memories.

I used to be that guy: no-drama believer of facts. I have since grown
better; I have been thrusted to more mature roles, *direk*. While
managing my own drama is an on-going thing, a recent win involved
de-escalating other people's shit-talking mouth, because that is a saner
thing to do, actually.
