## New work

Onboarding a new employee like me at New Work means ingesting a deluge
of new information previously irrelevant to my past lives, while staring
for at a computer screen for at least 8 hours. Exhaustion hit me like a
familiar foe. I am already reminded of my stance on both
[work](/anti-work) and [AI](/ai).

The term "productivity" is finding its way again in my orbit. It started
when I was trying to set up
[VimWiki](https://github.com/vimwiki/vimwiki) in Windows 11. I needed a
note-taking system that felt familiar and is reliable. For others, it is
the form of [Fathom](https://www.fathom.ai/) ("Never Take Notes Again"
is their tagline as of writing). For me, it is VimWiki.
