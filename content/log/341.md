The inside of head is a weird yet comfortable whirlpool to get sucked in
and never come out of. It is the same place that tunes out some details
that have become irrelevant, even when some people tell me they still
are.

This world is full of spins and spinners, even
secondhand news tell me that much. Nothing is stopping me to do that to
myself, definitely not the colorful brain scans of lives past.

But only the dead and liars can "lie still." Here and now, I have been
told, with all of its moving parts are far superior. And I agree.
