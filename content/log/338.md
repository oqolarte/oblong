Big city fires, left and right, break out these days as the month of
April *really* herald really hot summer days.

I have been busy with... work, mostly. And recuperating from work. The
latter comprises making peace with myself, but knowing about all the bad
things happening around us, can one sustain such practice? Thinking more
about it, it is hard to do so---to seek sustainable life, even---in an
unsustainable system.

Every waking moment, someone has some sand in their mouth. Same person
speaks of fiery rage. Glass tongues do not keep bad stuff at bay. The
most reasonable thing to do for now is to try to control what you can.
