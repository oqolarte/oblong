## Tie with the unseen

I fear many abstract things that do not endanger my life. For example, I
fear being irrelevant, even though, for a long while I had romanticized
going off-grid, far from the [chaos](/chaos) of modern life. Its
mildest version might be something like nobody gets my humor, and my
jokes keep failing to land. On the other extreme end,
isolation---specifically, people casting me out because I have stood for
my beliefs.
