## Routine

This is for oily skin. Please do your due diligence before trying this
routine yourself.

1. **Cleanser**: exfoliating cleanser, with gentle chemical exfoliant
2. **Serums**:
        1. Vitamin C, in the morning
        2. Retinol, in the evening before going to sleep
3. **Moisturizer**: either gel or lotion, twice a day, once in morning
   and once in evening
4. **Sunscreen**: should cover both UVA and UVB spectrum; SPF 50 or
   higher
