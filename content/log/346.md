## Sorting e-waste

I knew I have two 2GB DDR3 RAM sticks somewhere. Maybe I could use one
to boost teh RAM of one of the Thinkpads we have---only if I could
remember where I last put it.

When things become important and/or relevant, they suddenly "misplace
themselves." And I suddenly "forget" that it was me who moved it around.

Anyway, I found them eventually when I was sorting our e-waste to be
dropped off at a local state university for recycling.

The last twist to this story is, I cannot use the RAM sticks because the
Thinkpad (T440) only has one slot for RAM and it is currently being
used. Upgrading its RAM would mean replacing the current one.

## Thinking about video games

The reason why I want to bump the specs of this Thinkpad is because I
am, once again, thinking about playing video games.

The dilemma is about playing games in a higher spec'd [computer](/computer),
which means either buying a dedicated [secondhand] gaming rig, or
playing older games using laptops (like the Thinkpad) we already have
here. For the latter, since Thinkpads are not known for their gaming
capabilities, it would mean, for me, playing games in emulators (GBA,
PS1, maybe PS2).

## Myokimia

Or, you know, I should reduce my screentime, because of my [myokimia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Myokymia).

*Myokimia* is described as continuous and fine contractions that affect
the lower eyelid mainly, associated with stress, fatigue, and caffeine
intake.

I have had this as far back as 2015, perhaps even before that. It has
*always* been the lower eyelid of the left eye.
