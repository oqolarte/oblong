I am lucky to have a roof above my head in a big, big city full of
boring surprises. I am luckier, as I write this, to be in close
proximity to big trees even though I live in the heart of a dense urban
center. And yet majority of the beautiful places in my mind do not
include these very spaces I now inhabit. Perhaps that is what living in
Metro Manila for so long does.

Curiously, I am finding it hard to imagine settling in
transformed-into-tourist places and live there for good, because then I
would just add to a growing population not native there.

But truly, Metro Manila is exhausting. Propping it up also exhausts
everyone and everything else, but not necessarily all at the same time.
