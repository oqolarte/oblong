## At work

I do not feel useful in any corpo environment. Most of what I have been
doing is a constant veering away from being the burden of the group. Any
measurable "productive" work resulting from that is just bonus.

The space is just at odds with who I am now. And if more than half of
the bosses are toxic white immigrant men-children who exert little
effort in regulating their emotions, what is the incentive to do good
work? I am racking my head still for an outcome that I, too, might win
without having to cause any harm to anyone.

An aunt told me that Filipinos do not "love their craft". Do suffering
through menial jobs until one's pupils dilate even lead to loving such
"craft"? Does wasting away in front of a computer screen merit "loving"
it?

However, there is very little upside on getting tensed up. What I can
do, as what I have been trying to, is to manage my behavior around other
people.

*'Wag manakit. 'Wag paapi.*
